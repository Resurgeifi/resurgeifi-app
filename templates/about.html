💫 About Resurgifi

This isn't just an app. It's a comeback story.

Resurgifi is a self-guided recovery and mental health app built by Kevin Conzo and Eileen Lynch, born from real-life experience, grief, trauma, and healing. It’s designed to help users reconnect with clarity, calm, and personal transformation.

Unlike generic wellness tools, Resurgifi speaks from the inside out — weaving narrative, journaling, visual guidance, and AI-supported reflection into a daily practice of inner resurgence.

Core tools include:

  • 🌀 The Inner Circle — ask your Personal Circle of Resurgifi AIs anything
  • 📓 Journaling — write, reflect, and build your streak with real reward motivations (Merch!)
  • 🔥 Burnthrough — vent emotional weight through visual feeling prompts when words are lost
  • 🎯 Personalized journey onboarding to shape your RESURGENCE!

This is Kevin’s opus — a legacy for healing, created not from theory, but from living it.

“Stop the spiral, Resurgifi your life!”
