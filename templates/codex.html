🌌 The Inner Codex: Framework Behind Resurgifi

Resurgifi is a therapeutic storytelling platform designed to support emotional healing and recovery through immersive narrative structure, reflection, and guided journaling. What appears, at first glance, to be a comic-style recovery world is, in fact, a clinically informed inner landscape built to invite growth — and make it stick.

🧭 A Symbolic Recovery World

Inside the State of Inner, users explore a world shaped by metaphor — heroes that represent coping tools, villains that reflect relapse triggers, and zones of safety or chaos depending on their emotional state. The app doesn’t lecture. It reflects.

Each step a user takes — whether completing quests, engaging with characters, or journaling through pain — adds to a broader, trackable portrait of effort, insight, and healing-in-progress.

The design is grounded in narrative therapy, CBT, emotional regulation, and lived experience — but delivered in a way that doesn’t feel clinical.

📄 Professional Summaries

We’ve built a feature to help translate a user’s activity into something readable and useful for therapists, case managers, and counselors. These summaries analyze recent reflections, completed quests, and emotional tone — and distill it into a warm, narrative-style report.

It’s not a diagnostic tool. It’s a conversation starter. One that often reveals more than the client could put into words on their own.
